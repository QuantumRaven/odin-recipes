Shredded Chicken Burritos

Description

Burritos filled with slow-cooked shredded chicken, sour cream, refried beans, and Mexican-style rice. The chicken filling is also great for quesadillas and tacos.

Ingredients

  • 3 (5oz) frozen chicken breasts
  • ¼ cup salsa
  • 1 medium jalapeno pepper, diced
  • 1 dried red chile pepper
  • 1 (1oz) envelope taco seasoning mix (such as Ortega®)/li>
  • 1 cup uncooked white rice
  • 8 (10 in.) flour tortillas
  • 1 (16oz) can refried beans
  • ¼ cup shredded Cheddar cheese, or to taste
  • ¼ cup sour cream, or to taste

Steps

  1. Place chicken in the bottom of a slow cooker and add just enough water to cover, 2 1/2 to 3 cups. Mix in jalapeno, dried chile, garlic, and taco seasoning.
  2. Cook on Low until chicken is no longer pink in the center, juices run clear, and it shreds easily with a fork, about 5 hours.
  3. Shred chicken and allow to cook in the water mixture on Low, about 20 more minutes. Remove chicken from the pot and set aside in a bowl. Transfer cooking water to a saucepan.
  4. Add rice to the cooking water and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender and water has been absorbed, 20 to 25 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile, preheat the oven to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C).
  6. Wrap tortillas in foil and place in the preheated oven to warm, 5 to 10 minutes.
  7. At the same time, heat refried beans in a microwave-safe bowl in the microwave until warm, 1 to 2 minutes.
  8. Add beans, rice, chicken mixture, Cheddar cheese, and sour cream to each tortilla. Fold tortillas and serve.
