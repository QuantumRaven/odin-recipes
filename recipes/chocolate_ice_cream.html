Chocolate Ice Cream

Home

Description

The sweetest chocolate ice cream to ever land on your tastebuds.

Ingrediants

  • 1 cup milk
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 3 egg yolk, lightly beaten
  • 2 ounces semisweet chocolate, chopped
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Step 1: Gather all ingredients.
  2. Step 2: Combine milk, sugar, cocoa powder, and salt in a saucepan over medium heat. Bring to a simmer, stirring constantly.
  3. Step 3: Place egg yolks into a small bowl. Gradually whisk in about 1/2 cup of hot milk mixture, then return to the saucepan. Heat and stir until thickened, but do not boil.
  4. Step 4: Remove from the heat and stir in chopped chocolate until melted.
  5. Step 5: Pour into a chilled bowl and refrigerate, stirring occasionally, until cold, about 2 hours.
  6. Step 6: Stir in cream and vanilla. Pour into an ice cream maker and freeze according to manufacturer's directions.
